My Story

I'm a passionate student and aspiring cybersecurity professional with hands-on experience as a full-stack developer. My journey began with a curiosity about how websites work, which has grown into a love for crafting elegant, efficient, and user-friendly applications.

I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives. Whether it's building a responsive web application, optimizing database performance, or exploring cloud solutions, I approach every project with dedication and attention to detail.

Currently, my foundation is in full-stack development, which has given me a solid understanding of web technologies, databases, and application architecture. However, my ultimate goal is to specialize in cybersecurity, where I aim to protect systems, networks, and data from evolving threats, identify vulnerabilities, and help organizations strengthen their digital defenses.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or learning about cybersecurity trends and best practices. I'm always eager to grow and make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.
